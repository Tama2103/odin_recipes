Rendang Recipes

Ingredients:

  • 500 gr rendang meat
  • packets instant rendang seasoning (mine: bamboe)
  • 1 bks instant coconut milk
  • 2 lemongrass stalks
  • 4 orange leaves
  • 2 cm galangal
  • 00 mL stock water (meat stew)
  • to taste Salt
  • to taste Sugar
  • to taste Broth powder

Ground spices:

  • 6 garlic cloves
  • 10 cloves shallots
  • 2 red chilies
  • 8 cayenne pepper

How To Make:

  1. Prepare the ingredients.
  2. Cut the beef into pieces and boil it using the 15.30.15.30 method to save gas.
  3. Saut√© ground spices, lemongrass, lime leaves, galangal until fragrant. Add instant rendang seasoning, mix well.
  4. Pour in the meat cooking water. Add the meat, add coconut milk, seasoning (pepper, salt, stock powder). Then correct the flavor. If it is right, cook until the coconut milk is reduced. Rendang is ready

Meatball Recipes

Ingredients:

  • 25 seeds
  • 500 gr Scotch beef fillet (I minced beef)
  • 70 gr tapioca (about 5 tbsp)
  • 100 gr crushed ice (about 8 tbsp)
  • 2 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp pepper
  • 2 cloves garlic puree
  • 1 egg white
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 3000 ml water for boiling meatballs
Gravy Ingredients:
  • 2500 ml water (take from the soup to boil the meatballs)
  • 1 chunk of garlic (11-12 cloves)
  • 1 whole celery stalk
  • 1 whole spring onion
  • 1 tbsp sugar
How To Make
  1. Prepare all ingredients
  2. Cut the destination meat to make it easier to puree. Place in a food processor, grind together with crushed ice until smooth. Add egg white, tapioca, salt, pepper, and garlic. Grind again until well mixed and really smooth. Nb. Put it in the refrigerator for 30-45 minutes to make it chewier.
  3. Bring water to a boil, then reduce heat
  4. Take a round shape of dough, put it in the palm of your hand and then grasp it between your thumb and forefinger. Put it into the water using a spoon.
  5. Saute the spices, add sugar, salt and stock powder. Take the remaining 2500ml meatball water and pour it into the stir-fry seasoning. Add celery and spring onion. Let it boil to taste
  6. Arrange noodles in a bowl, meatballs on top, then pakcoy / mustard greens, spring onions, celery, sauce and fried onions. Add chili sauce, soy sauce and ketchup.
